Quick answers before launch

Good questions.
Clear answers.

Everything you need to know before your first sign-in.

Do I need to re-enroll?

No. Use your existing username and password for your first sign-in, then follow the prompts to create a new password.

Do I need a new app?

Update the Penn East Mobile App to the latest version. Turning on automatic updates is the easiest way to be ready.

Will my account numbers or Bill Pay change?

Your account and routing numbers stay the same. Existing Bill Pay payees and recurring payments will transfer.

Will my eStatements transfer?

Yes. Your eStatement enrollment stays in place, so no action is required.

Will I need to set up Face ID again?

Yes. Re-enable Face ID or Fingerprint Login after your first successful sign-in.

Can I connect accounts from other institutions?

Yes. Eligible external accounts can be connected so you can view more of your financial picture in one dashboard.

Is Digital Banking secure?

The new experience uses secure identity verification and modern tools designed to help protect access to your financial information.
