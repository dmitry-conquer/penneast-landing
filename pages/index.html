Why we're upgrading

Designed around your feedback.

Designed around your feedback A better Digital Banking experience

At Penn East, we're always looking for ways to improve the way you bank.

Over the past year, we've listened to member feedback, reviewed survey responses, and evaluated how members use Online and Mobile Banking every day. Your feedback helped shape our decision to invest in a modern Digital Banking platform that delivers the features, convenience, and security our members expect.

The result is a faster, more intuitive experience with powerful tools designed to help you manage your finances with confidence. From a personalized dashboard and improved navigation to enhanced card controls and financial wellness tools, everything has been thoughtfully designed with our members in mind.

Before launch day

Are you
launch ready?

To help make your first login as smooth as possible, complete these three simple steps before August 25.

01

Know your username and password

You'll use your existing username and password the first time you sign in. After successfully logging in, you'll be prompted to create a new password before accessing your new Digital Banking experience.

02

Verify your contact information

Make sure your email address and mobile phone number are up to date. We'll use this information to securely verify your identity during your first login.

03

Turn on automatic app updates

If you use the Penn East Mobile App, enable automatic app updates so you'll automatically receive the newest version when it's available.

Frequently asked questions

Quick Answers
Before Launch

Getting Started

Do I need to re-enroll?

You'll use your existing username and password the first time you sign in.

Do I need a new app?

If you use the Penn East Mobile App, enable automatic app updates so you'll automatically receive the newest version when it's available.

What happens the first time I log in?

After successfully logging in with your current username and password, you'll be prompted to create a new password before accessing your new Digital Banking experience.

Why do I need to verify my email?

We'll use your up-to-date email address and mobile phone number to securely verify your identity during your first login.

Your Accounts

Will my account numbers change?

Your account numbers and routing number are staying exactly the same.

Will my eStatements transfer?

Your eStatement enrollment is staying the same. No action is required.

Will Bill Pay transfer?

Your Bill Pay payees and recurring payments are staying the same. Existing payees and recurring payments will transfer.

Will my alerts transfer?

Set up new alerts after launch.

Can I connect external accounts?

Securely connect eligible accounts from other financial institutions and view balances alongside your Penn East accounts in one convenient dashboard.

Security

Will I need to set up Face ID again?

Re-enable Face ID or Fingerprint Login after your first login.

How does Digital Snapshot work?

After enabling Digital Snapshot, quickly view your Penn East account balances without signing in.

Is Digital Banking secure?

The modern Digital Banking platform delivers the features, convenience, and security our members expect.
